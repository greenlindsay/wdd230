Site Purpose

the purpose of a chamber of commerce is to provide an organization that will help to strengthen business relationships amongst local business owners, employees and professionals of all kinds of businesses. The point of my Orem Chamber website will provide a source for the citizens of Orem to visit and learn more about the Chamber itself and its mission, which businesses are a part of the organization, and events and ways to get involved.
