Welcome to our page!

Scoots is a scooter and vehicle rental service designed to cater to locals, walk-in tourists, motor scooter enthusiasts, and cruise-based touring groups and partnerships to explore the beautiful island of Cozumel, Mexico!

Scoots has two locations: the el Centro de Cozumel location near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock, and the other near the Terminal Puerta Maya along the coast which caters to cruise lines and patrons.

We offer a range of different scooter models as well as a few motor vehicles. Our available models are:

  • Honda Metro Scooter
  • Honda Dio Scooter
  • Honda PCX150 Scooter
  • Jeep Wrangler - 4-door with A/C
  • Jeep Wrangler - 2-door
