Design

For the design of my Chamber of Commerce website, I really wanted the color and font themes to be sophisticated and classy, since this is an official city organization that needs to have a professional feel. I chose navy/dark blue as my main color with other blue shades and neutral colors for the majority of the site. I want my typography font to be something classy, similar to Times New Roman but less generic.
